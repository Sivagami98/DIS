Our History

Jashn-e-desi was founded in 2020 by 4 enthusiastic youngsters, who had a passion for creating memorable and impactful events. Starting with a small team of 4, the company focused on providing end-to-end event management services to corporate clients in India.

In the early years, the company faced several challenges including fierce competition and a lack of brand recognition. However, with a focus on delivering high-quality events and building strong relationships with clients, Jashn-e-desi Event Management gradually grew in size and reputation.

By 2022, the company had expanded its service offerings to include weddings and social events. This move proved to be successful and helped the company gain a foothold in the highly competitive wedding planning market in India.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create unforgettable events that exceed our clients' expectations. We strive to bring creativity, innovation, and attention to detail to every project we undertake. We aim to provide exceptional customer service and personalized solutions that cater to the unique needs of each client. Our goal is to be recognized as a leading event management company that consistently delivers high-quality events and establishes long-term partnerships with our clients.

Our Values

Creativity: We value creative solutions that engage and inspire audiences, and help our clients stand out from the competition.

Integrity: We believe in doing business with honesty, transparency, and fairness. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients and partners based on trust and respect.

Excellence: We are committed to delivering high-quality events that meet or exceed our clients' expectations. We pay attention to every detail and aim to exceed standards in everything we do.

Collaboration: We believe that teamwork and collaboration are essential to creating successful events. We work closely with our clients and partners to ensure that everyone is on the same page and working towards the same goals.

Innovation: We are always looking for new and innovative ways to deliver exceptional events. We embrace new technologies and techniques that can help us create memorable experiences for our clients and their audiences.
